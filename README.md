# Smart Research Intelligent System

## Overview

Smart Research Intelligent System is an AI-powered Research Agent developed using IBM Granite Models, IBM watsonx.ai, and LangFlow.

The system helps researchers, students, and professionals streamline research activities by automating literature review, information retrieval, summarization, and report generation. It acts as an intelligent research assistant that improves productivity and accelerates knowledge discovery.

---

## Problem Statement

Research activities often require extensive time and effort to search for academic papers, review literature, extract key insights, and organize information from multiple sources.

The Smart Research Intelligent System addresses these challenges by providing an intelligent AI-powered solution capable of understanding research queries, generating summaries, extracting key findings, and creating structured research reports.

---

## System Workflow

1. User submits a research query.
2. LangFlow receives the request.
3. IBM watsonx.ai processes the query.
4. IBM Granite analyzes and generates insights.
5. Research summaries and findings are produced.
6. Results are displayed to the user.
